Goals and purpose?

stream aims to be Entertaining for the viewers to enjoy watching, and make themselves feel connected with the streaming experience. The stream seeks to be Engaging for the viewers to have fun and express themselves by interacting, with the use of live chat, and have a reflection on what we have learned from our overall experiences in AI. We are here also to Inform the probable impact on those who use AI technology and let them know what AI could bring students or themselves as they use this technology with no caution.

Promotional plan

1. Pre-Stream Promotions

a. Social Media Teasers Platform: Facebook Content Ideas: Countdown posts with eye-catching visuals like “Can AI be helpful for Apec students?” AI-related trivia posts to spark curiosity in the viewers.

b. Design Promo Graphics Design posters with the following details: Event Title: "AI or Nah? Let’s Find Out!” Date/Time: [date], 2:00–3:35 PM Platform: Facebook Live

c. Hashtags Use hashtags like #AIChallenge, #DiscoverAI, and #FunWithAI to increase visibility.

2. Day of the Stream Promotions

a. Reminder Posts Post reminders on Facebook 2 hours and 30 minutes before the stream starts. Include a direct link to the stream.

b. Stories Update Share an Instagram and Facebook story with the caption: “We’re going live soon! Join us for fun and prizes!”
